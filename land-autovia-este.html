Description

Exceptional 75,000 m² development parcel located along the new 4-lane highway currently under construction, which will connect the Punta Cana Circunvalación to the Autovía del Este near the toll area. The property has direct frontage on the new road — a key commercial and development advantage once the highway is fully operational.

At the rear of the lot, a natural cliff (farallón) provides elevated views and additional development possibilities. An adjacent project is already being developed on the same cliff zone, confirming the area's growth trajectory.

The owner is willing to segregate 48,000 m² according to the buyer's needs, making this an unusually flexible opportunity for residential, mixed-use, or commercial development. The surrounding area includes Larimar City and several other residential projects currently in progress.

The road is actively under construction and the land is fully accessible for on-site visits.
