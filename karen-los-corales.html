Description

Stylish, fully-furnished 1-bedroom apartment in the heart of Los Corales, Bavaro — one of Punta Cana's most popular and walkable neighborhoods. Just a 5-minute walk from the beach, surrounded by restaurants, shops, and nightlife.

This ground-floor unit features a modern open-concept layout with a wood-accented living area, fully equipped kitchen with granite countertops, a spacious bedroom with king bed and smart TV, and a private terrace with comfortable outdoor furniture overlooking the tropical gardens.

The complex offers a resort-style pool, covered BBQ and lounge area, on-site gym, manicured gardens, and 24/7 security. An ideal property for personal use, vacation rental investment, or both — Los Corales consistently delivers strong Airbnb occupancy year-round.
