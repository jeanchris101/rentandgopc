## WHATSAPP / TELEGRAM

WhatsApp groups are the primary communication channel for DR real estate transactions. They are typically:
- **Invite-only** — join through real estate agents, property managers, or other groups
- **Agent-run** — most are created by real estate agencies to share listings
- **Location-specific** — separate groups for Cap Cana, Bavaro, Cocotal, etc.

**How to find them:**
1. Ask DR real estate agents directly (they all have groups)
2. Join Facebook groups above and look for WhatsApp invite links
3. Attend InterNations or expat meetups in DR
4. Connect with property managers on the ground

**Telegram**: Less common for DR real estate specifically. No major public channels found. The market primarily uses WhatsApp.

## POSTING STRATEGY BY PLATFORM

### Content Types That Work Best

| Platform | Best Content | Frequency | Tone |
|----------|-------------|-----------|------|
| **FB Real Estate Groups** | Listings with photos, price comparisons, market updates | 2-3x/week | Professional, informative |
| **FB Expat Groups** | Area guides, cost of living, lifestyle tips | 1-2x/week | Helpful, community-oriented |
| **FB French Groups** | French-language guides, Quebec-specific info (flights, banking) | 1-2x/week | Friendly, relatable |
| **FB Russian Group** | Russian-language property highlights, visa/residency info | 1x/week | Informative |
| **Reddit** | Data-driven answers to questions, yield analysis | As questions arise | Helpful, no self-promo |
| **DR1 Forum** | Expert answers to property questions, market insights | 2-3x/week | Authoritative |
| **LinkedIn** | Market analysis articles, investment ROI breakdowns | 1x/week | Professional |
| **YouTube** | Property tours, neighborhood guides, ROI calculators | 1-2x/month | Engaging, educational |

### Rules to Follow
1. **No spam** — provide value first, promote second
2. **Read group rules** before posting (many ban direct listings)
3. **Engage first** — comment on others' posts for 1-2 weeks before posting your own content
4. **Localize** — use French in Quebec groups, Russian in Russian group, Spanish where appropriate
5. **Share data** — groups love market stats, yield numbers, price comparisons
6. **Answer questions** — position yourself as the knowledgeable expert
7. **Link subtly** — link to your site's market-intel or ROI calculator pages as "resources" rather than sales pages
