CONFOTUR Tax Benefits Included

The CONFOTUR Law (Law 158-01) grants 15-year tax exemptions to tourism projects in DR. This property qualifies:

  • 0% transfer tax (saves ~3% of price)
  • 0% property tax (IPI) for 15 years
  • 0% capital gains tax
  • 0% rental income tax
  • ITBIS exemption on construction materials
  • Import tax exemption
