Pricing Strategy Recommendations
Optimal nightly rates by zone, season, and property type.
Length-of-stay discounts: Offer -10% for 7+ nights, -20% for 14+ nights, and -25% for 28+ nights (monthly). Long stays reduce turnover costs and cleaning fees significantly.
Last-minute pricing: Drop rates 15-20% for bookings within 72 hours of check-in. An occupied night at a discount beats an empty night every time.
Weekend premium: Friday/Saturday nights command 10-20% more than weekday rates in most beach zones. Set up smart pricing rules accordingly.
Minimum stay strategy: Set 3-night minimums during high season, 2-night in shoulder, and 1-night in low season. During Christmas/New Year, enforce 5-7 night minimums for maximum revenue.
