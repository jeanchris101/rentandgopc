## 1. AUDIENCE SEGMENTS

### Segment A: USA Buyers (English)
- **Demographics**: Ages 35-65, HHI $75K+, homeowners, college educated
- **Geo**: East Coast heavy — NYC metro, Miami-Dade, Boston, Atlanta, Charlotte, Philadelphia
- **Interests**: Caribbean travel, real estate investing, Airbnb hosting, passive income, retirement planning
- **Behaviors**: Frequent international travelers, engaged shoppers
- **Mindset**: Looking for ROI, lifestyle upgrade, or retirement plan. Compares to Miami/Florida pricing.

### Segment B: Dominican Diaspora (Spanish)
- **Demographics**: Ages 28-55, Dominican-American
- **Geo**: NYC metro (Bronx, Washington Heights, Inwood), Miami-Dade, Boston metro, NJ (Essex, Passaic, Hudson)
- **Interests**: Dominican Republic, Punta Cana, bachata, vuelos a Santo Domingo, remesas
- **Language**: Spanish primary, bilingual for 2nd gen
- **Mindset**: Emotional — pride, family legacy, "tener lo tuyo en tu tierra." Price-sensitive but motivated by payment plans.

### Segment C: Quebec/Canada (French)
- **Demographics**: Ages 40-65, snowbird culture, homeowners
- **Geo**: Montreal (YUL), Quebec City (YQB), Laval, Longueuil, Gatineau
- **Interests**: Voyages dans le sud, Republique dominicaine, immobilier, Sunwing, Air Transat
- **Language**: Quebec French (use "tu" not "vous", "condo" not "appartement", "jaser" not "parler")
- **Mindset**: Practical. Already goes to DR in winter. Tired of paying for all-inclusives. Wants a property that pays for itself.

### Segment D: LatAm Investors (Neutral Spanish)
- **Sub-segments**: Colombian, Venezuelan, Argentine buyers
- **Demographics**: Ages 30-55, professionals/business owners
- **Geo**: Miami (already relocated), Bogota, Medellin, Buenos Aires, Caracas diaspora
- **Interests**: Inversiones en dolares, bienes raices internacional, diversificacion patrimonial
- **Mindset**: Capital preservation in USD. Sophisticated — they ask about cap rates, IRR, and comparable analysis. Skeptical of Caribbean RE due to regional scam reputation.

### Segment E: European / Russian-speaking
- **Demographics**: Ages 35-60, upper-middle class
- **Geo**: Moscow, St. Petersburg, Russian diaspora in Miami/NYC/Berlin/Dubai
- **Interests**: International real estate, investment immigration, luxury lifestyle
- **Language**: Russian
- **Mindset**: Asset diversification, lifestyle purchase, potential residency pathway. Familiar with resort-style developments.

## 3. CAMPAIGN STRUCTURE: AWARENESS > CONSIDERATION > CONVERSION

### STAGE 1: AWARENESS (Top of Funnel)
**Objective**: Video Views / Reach
**Budget**: 40% of total ad spend
**Duration**: Ongoing

- **Content**: Property walkthrough videos (60-90 sec), drone footage, lifestyle reels (beach, golf, Melia resort), "Day in the Life" clips
- **Targeting**: Broad interest-based targeting per segment (see Segment definitions above)
- **Platforms**: Instagram Reels, Facebook Feed, Facebook Watch
- **KPI**: Video views (50%+ completion), reach, CPM
- **Optimization**: Optimize for ThruPlay (15-second views)

**Ad formats**:
- 15-30 sec Reels (vertical, subtitled, music)
- 60-90 sec Facebook video (property tour)
- Carousel: 5 images (golf view, terrace, Melia beach, interior, aerial)

### STAGE 2: CONSIDERATION (Middle of Funnel)
**Objective**: Lead Generation / Traffic
**Budget**: 35% of total ad spend
**Duration**: Ongoing, retargeting Stage 1 viewers

- **Audience**: Custom audience of people who watched 50%+ of Stage 1 videos + website visitors
- **Content**: Lead magnets — "Punta Cana Buyer's Guide" PDF, cost breakdown infographic, "CONFOTUR Explained" carousel, price comparison (vs Miami, vs all-inclusive)
- **Platforms**: Facebook Lead Ads, Instagram Stories, Facebook Feed
- **KPI**: Cost per lead (CPL), landing page CTR
- **Lead capture**: Facebook native lead form (name, email, phone, WhatsApp)

**Ad formats**:
- Facebook Lead Ads (instant form, no landing page friction)
- Carousel: "5 Things Americans Don't Know About Buying in DR"
- Single image with strong headline + lead form CTA

**Lead magnets by segment**:
- USA: "The American's Guide to Buying in Punta Cana" (PDF)
- Diaspora: "Guia Completa: Como Comprar en Punta Cana Desde USA" (PDF)
- Quebec: "Guide pour acheteurs quebecois — Immobilier a Punta Cana" (PDF)
- LatAm: "Inversion en Punta Cana: Analisis Completo" (PDF)
- Russian: "Инвестиции в Punta Cana: Полное руководство" (PDF)

### STAGE 3: CONVERSION (Bottom of Funnel)
**Objective**: Conversions (WhatsApp message / consultation booking)
**Budget**: 25% of total ad spend
**Duration**: Ongoing, retargeting Stage 2 leads

- **Audience**: People who downloaded the guide, visited property page 2+ times, engaged with 3+ posts
- **Content**: Direct property ads with price, CTA to WhatsApp or booking page. Testimonials from buyers. Urgency messaging ("Last unit with this view").
- **Platforms**: Facebook Feed, Instagram Feed, WhatsApp Click-to-Chat ads
- **KPI**: Cost per qualified lead, cost per WhatsApp conversation started
- **CTA**: "Send WhatsApp Message" or "Book a Free Consultation Call"

**Ad formats**:
- Click-to-WhatsApp ads (highest intent, lowest friction)
- Single image: property hero shot + price + direct CTA
- Video testimonial from a buyer (30-60 sec)
- Retargeting carousel showing the specific property they viewed

## 4. BUDGET RECOMMENDATIONS (Small Team, 2-5 People)

### Monthly Budget Tiers

**STARTER: $500-800/month**
- Focus on 2 segments only (USA + Diaspora or Diaspora + Quebec)
- $300-500 on Stage 1 (awareness/video views)
- $150-200 on Stage 2 (lead generation)
- $50-100 on Stage 3 (retargeting/conversion)
- Expected: 30-60 leads/month, 3-8 qualified conversations

**GROWTH: $1,500-2,500/month**
- Cover 3-4 segments
- $600-1,000 on Stage 1
- $500-800 on Stage 2
- $400-700 on Stage 3
- Expected: 80-150 leads/month, 10-20 qualified conversations

**SCALE: $3,000-5,000/month**
- All 5 segments
- Full funnel with lookalike audiences
- A/B testing ad variations
- Expected: 150-300 leads/month, 25-50 qualified conversations

### Budget Allocation by Segment (Growth tier example)
- Dominican Diaspora: 35% (highest volume, lowest CPL, most emotional buyers)
- USA Buyers: 25% (higher CPL but higher deal value)
- Quebec/Canada: 20% (seasonal — increase to 35% in Aug-Oct)
- LatAm Investors: 15% (niche but serious when they convert)
- Russian-speaking: 5% (test budget, scale if ROI proves out)

### Key Timing
- **Dominican Diaspora**: Year-round, peak around holidays (Dec) and summer (people visiting RD)
- **USA Buyers**: Year-round, slight peak in Jan-Mar (New Year resolutions, tax season thinking)
- **Quebec**: HEAVY spend Aug-Oct (planning winter escape). Secondary Jan-Feb (already in DR, upgrade mindset)
- **LatAm**: Year-round, peak when local currencies weaken (watch ARS, COP news)
- **Russian**: Year-round, test and learn

## KEY PRINCIPLES

1. **Lead with lifestyle, ROI, and location** — NOT tax benefits. CONFOTUR is a nice bonus to mention, not the headline.
2. **"Try Before You Buy"** is your strongest differentiator — invite serious leads to visit.
3. **WhatsApp is king** — especially for Diaspora, LatAm, and Russian segments. Make it the primary CTA.
4. **Social proof converts** — every closing photo, every testimonial, every Airbnb income screenshot builds trust.
5. **Don't hard-sell in groups** — provide value (education, tips, honest answers) and the leads will come.
6. **Speak their language** — literally (translations) and culturally (Quebec French, Dominican Spanish, neutral LatAm Spanish).
7. **Seasonal spending** — increase Quebec budget Aug-Oct, Diaspora budget Nov-Dec, keep USA steady.
