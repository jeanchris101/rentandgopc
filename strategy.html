4 Fix Listing #7 (4.78 Rating)

▼
Diagnostic Checklist
  • Review all sub-4-star reviews from last 6 months, identify recurring complaints (cleanliness? check-in? accuracy?)
  • Check average response time in Airbnb inbox, must be under 1 hour for Superhost
  • Verify all listing photos are current, no outdated furniture, missing items, or misleading angles
  • Compare pricing vs. top 5 competitors within 1km, are you overpriced for what you deliver?
  • Audit listing description for accuracy, every promised amenity must be present and working
  • Check cleaning crew consistency, same team every time, use a checklist with photos
  • Test all appliances, AC units, water pressure, WiFi speed, log the results
  • Review check-in process, is it smooth? Self-check-in with lockbox or smart lock?
5-Step Action Plan: 4.78 → 4.9+
1
Deep Clean & Refresh

Professional deep clean. Replace stained linens, worn towels, scratched cookware. Add new mattress topper. First impressions drive 60% of the rating. Budget: $200-400 one-time.

2
Upgrade the Welcome Experience

Printed welcome guide (WiFi, local restaurants, emergency numbers). Small welcome basket (water, snacks, local coffee). Handwritten "Welcome!" card. These drive 5-star "check-in" scores.

3
Proactive Messaging Sequence

Day 1 message: "How's everything? Need anything?", catches problems early before they become bad reviews. Day before checkout: "Anything we can improve?", gives them a vent channel that isn't the review.

4
Re-Shoot Photos

Hire a local photographer ($50-100). Shoot at golden hour. Stage the space: fresh flowers, made bed, set dining table. Update all listing photos. Better photos = better-matched guests = better reviews.

5
Price Down Temporarily for Review Volume

Drop price 15% for 4-6 weeks to attract more bookings. More stays = more reviews = dilute old low scores faster. After 10+ new 5-star reviews, gradually return to normal pricing.

40% discount for first 5 bookings: Airbnb's "New listing promotion" setting handles this automatically. Enable it in Pricing > Promotions. This stacks with your manual pricing ramp, first guests get the steepest deal, which maximizes booking velocity and early reviews.
New Listing Boost Checklist
  • Complete 100% of Airbnb profile fields, every field filled gives search ranking boost
  • Enable Instant Book, Airbnb heavily favors instant-book listings in search results
  • Set 2-night minimum (not 3) for first month, lower barrier = more bookings = more reviews faster
  • Professional photos, 20+ photos covering every room, bathroom, view, building exterior, pool, neighborhood
  • Write a compelling description with local keywords: "Bavaro", "Punta Cana", "beach", "pool", "airport"
  • Add all amenities, even obvious ones (AC, hot water, hangers). More checked = better search match
  • Set up automated messages: booking confirmation, check-in instructions (24h before), mid-stay check-in, checkout reminder
  • Calendar open for at least 3 months ahead, Airbnb penalizes short availability windows
  • Share listing link on personal social media, first external traffic signals help ranking
